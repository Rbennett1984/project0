Yogic Yama English Name Practice Tip
AHIMSA Nonviolence Practice being more kind, accepting, and forgiving of yourself and others.
SATYA Truthfulness Refrain from telling lies and speak with kindness, compassion, and clarity.
ASTEYA Non-Stealing Because the urge to steal arises from a sense of unhappiness, incompleteness, and envy, the solution is to practice giving any chance you get.
BRAHMACHARYA Energy Conservation Making wise choices about the books and magazines you read, the movies you see, and the company you keep will help you conserve energy and keep your mind focused and dynamic.
APARIGRAHA Non-Possessiveness The practice of non-possessiveness helps us to examine our assumptions and guides us back to healthy relationships with others.
